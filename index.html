Consumer Reports · US

380,000 vehicle survey

Annual US member survey covering 20 problem areas across 2000–2026 model years. Strongest for brands sold in volume in North America. Misses Peugeot, Renault, Dacia, Fiat, Mini, Alfa Romeo due to insufficient US sample size.

What Car? · UK

30,000 UK owner survey

Annual UK survey across 31 brands with ~30k responses. Best source for Mini, Suzuki, Alfa Romeo, Jaguar and MG — brands with minimal US presence. Scores weight not just fault rates but repair costs and downtime.

TÜV Report · Europe

9.5M German MOT inspections

The largest dataset by far — mandatory annual technical inspections across Germany. Covers European brands comprehensively. Key finding 2026: Tesla Model Y posted the worst defect rate in a decade among near-new cars.
