Consumer Reports · US

380,000 vehicle survey

Annual US member survey covering 20 problem areas across 2000–2026 model years. Strongest for brands sold in volume in North America. Misses Peugeot, Renault, Dacia, Fiat, Mini, Alfa Romeo due to insufficient US sample size.

What Car? · UK

30,000 UK owner survey

Annual UK survey across 31 brands with ~30k responses. Publishes brand reliability as a percentage (76–98% range), rescaled to 0–100 here. Best source for Mini, Suzuki, Alfa Romeo, Jaguar and MG — brands with minimal US presence.

TÜV Report · Europe

9.5M German MOT inspections

The largest dataset by far — mandatory technical inspections across Germany. Publishes per-model defect rates (%), aggregated to brand level and inverted to 0–100 here. Tesla Model Y posted the worst defect rate in a decade (17.3%) among 2–3 year old cars.

Longevity data

iSeeCars + Junk Car Medics

X-axis km values derived from iSeeCars Longest-Lasting Brands study (250M+ vehicle analysis) and Junk Car Medics end-of-life data (50k junked vehicles, 2024). Original mile figures converted at ×1.609.
